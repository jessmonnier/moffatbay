About Us

Moffat Bay Lodge is a cozy island retreat offering guests a relaxing escape surrounded by natural beauty and coastal charm. Located near scenic hiking trails, kayaking routes, whale watching areas, and scuba diving sites, the lodge provides the perfect balance of comfort and adventure. With thoughtfully designed rooms, modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, and a welcoming atmosphere, Moffat Bay Lodge serves as an ideal home base for travelers looking to unwind, explore the outdoors, and experience the peaceful charm of island life.
